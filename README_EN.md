# Large Mart Customer RFM Analysis Project

Using **2 years of customer purchase data**, I performed **RFM analysis** to segment customers and identify the core group that contributes the most to revenue, then proposed targeted marketing strategies.

## Key Details
- **Data**: 2 years of customer transaction records  
- **Method**: RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)  
  - Recency: Days since last purchase  
  - Frequency: Purchase count  
  - Monetary: Total spend  
- **Process**  
  1. Data exploration & preprocessing  
  2. RFM metric calculation  
  3. Scoring and customer segmentation  
  4. Revenue contribution & characteristics analysis per segment  
- **Goal**: Maximize marketing ROI by targeting high-value customers with limited resources
